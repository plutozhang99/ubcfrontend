What does "standing" refer to?
Standing refers to your status in a particular course. It may refer to the grade you received, or any special status you may have.
 Under what session will my distance education grades appear?
The distance education grade will appear in the session in which the course was completed.

 Under what session will my supplemental exam grades appear?
The supplemental grade will appear as a separate course (called supplemental) in the same session as the original course.
